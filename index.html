Lasanga

Ingredents

  1. Pasta
  2. Cheese
  3. Mince

Process

There are two ways of doing this depending on if your using pasta sheets or corkscrew pasta.

Corkscrew Pasta

Put the mince in the pasta bake pan that you are using for the Lasanga mix the pasta with the mince the cover the top with cheese and bake.

Pasta Sheets

Layer the pasta sheets with mince inbetween maybe mix in some cheese. cover the top with cheese and bake.
